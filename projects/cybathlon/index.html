Cybathlon 2024

Vision Assistance Race

Robotics Prosthetics Assistive Tech

Developed an actuation system to assist passive polycentric knee users (such as the Kadam knee) in performing demanding tasks such as stair climbing. This project involved the design and prototyping of a cable-driven mechanism, which was presented at Cybathlon 2024.
