nDTAg

Geometry-based Clustering

Geometry Clustering Algorithms

This was initially a course project for the course "Algorithms in Computational Geometry" at IIT Madras. The project started off as a planning problem, including a 2D implementation of a Delaunay triangulation-based clustering algorithm in order to solve the Freeze-Tag problem, which tries to optimize time taken to "awaken" all robots in a swarm robotics scenario. After the course, under the guidance of Prof. Ramanathan Muthuganapathy, my classmate Kanish and I extended the project to higher dimensions and improved the algorithm to handle different types of data. We performed an analysis of the algorithm's performance compared to other standard clustering algorithms, and presented our findings as a poster at the International Conference for Geometric Modelling & Processing (GMP) 2025.
