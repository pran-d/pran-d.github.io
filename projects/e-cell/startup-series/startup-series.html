The Starting Up Machine

Our Vision

Startup Series is a program that aims to build, nurture and grow the startup ecosystem in the institute. It aims to provide firm support to early-stage idea-phased startups.

What is Startup Series?

E-Cell IIT Madras is delighted to announce the Startup Series, a 6-week-long program that aims to help aspiring entrepreneurs of insti take their ideas and ventures to the next level through a structured framework. Comprising interactive and instructional sessions, Startup Series will help take your product to the market, providing the participants with the skill and the insight to apply these techniques in building and validating their ideas.

Development

Build your idea and transform it into a scalable business model

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are eligible to participate in Startup Series?

Anyone with an idea and passion to drive that idea ahead is welcome to register. The shortlisting would be done on basis of the quality of submission. (Only IITM students can join)

Are there any earlier events that I needed to join?

No. Startup Series is an independent event, and there is no priority given to any team.

How many teams will be shortlisted?

There is no upper limit on the number of teams to be shortlisted. It will depend entirely on the quality of submissions received.

How many participants can be there in a team? Can we register individually?

A team can consist of 1-5 members. Individual registrations are also accepted.
